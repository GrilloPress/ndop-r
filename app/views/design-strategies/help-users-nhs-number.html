NHS numbers are not well-known identifiers.

Validation errors for asking users for their NHS numbers should be as helpful as possible. We should do the hard work to make it simple for users.

We have different ways we handle validation errors when we ask users to enter their NHS number.

Validation errors for enter your NHS number

  • No number
  • NHS number contains letters
  • Entry is in National Insurance number format
  • Too short
  • Too long
