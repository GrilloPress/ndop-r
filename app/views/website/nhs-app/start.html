Use this service to:

  • choose if your confidential patient information is shared for research and planning
  • change or check your current choice

Your choice will be applied by:

  • NHS Digital and Public Health England
  • all other health and care organisations by March 2020

Any choice you make will not change your individual care.

Before you start

You must:

  • be aged 13 or older
  • have access to your email or mobile phone
  • have your NHS number
    • or
  • your postcode registered with your GP surgery
