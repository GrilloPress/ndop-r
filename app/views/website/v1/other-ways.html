Make a choice for yourself

You can make or change your choice:

  • by phone
    • or
  • by print and post

Make your choice by phone

Speak to the NHS Digital Contact Centre. They can help you use the online service or make a choice on your behalf. You’ll need to confirm your identity.

NHS Digital Contact Centre
0300 303 5678
Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm excluding bank holidays

Make your choice by print and post

Use this form to make your choice. You’ll need to complete it and return it to us by post.

Make a choice for someone else

You can make or change a choice by post for:

  • your children under the age of 13
  • another person who cannot manage their choice themselves

Make a choice for your children

You can make or change a choice by post for children under the age of 13.

You must be the parent or legal guardian.

Manage another person’s choice on their behalf

You can make or change a choice by post for another person who cannot manage their choice themselves.

You must have the legal authority to do this. For example, if you have power of attorney.
